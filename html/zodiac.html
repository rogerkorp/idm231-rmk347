The Ethereal Diner

Can I take your order?
Welcome to the Ethereal Diner! Here you can order some of the finest foods in the galaxy. To place and order, simply press one of the buttons below, or let us decide the food for you based on your birthday!

How To Use This Website

  • Step 1: Enter your birthday!
    Click on the date entry box to either manually select your birthday, or type it in using mm/dd/yyyy formatting.
  • Step 2: Click the "Place Your Order" button!
    This will submit the birthday you entered. Make sure it's in a real date though, otherwise the order won't go through!
  • Step 3: Read your sign!
    This website will automatically update to show you information about your sign. If you would like to see the others, click on the corresponding buttons below!
